About

Skin Cancer is most commonly caused by cumulative exposure to the sun's UV rays. Early detection is crucial as treatment is more effective in the beginning stages of skin cancer. At later stages, treatment costs increase exponentially as procedures become more complex.

Although it is one of the easiest cancers to identify, many people ignore the issue as they do not recognise the symptoms.

With UV levels rising globally, it is crucial to tap into AI to help classify skin lesions accurately and help patients and doctors identify skin cancers early.

Recommendations

Physicians reccomend checking your skin spots every 3 months. With our skin checker, you can simply upload your photo to get the result instantanously!

Remember to apply UV protection on exposed skin or wear long sleeves whenever you go outdoors to reduce your exposure to UV rays!
